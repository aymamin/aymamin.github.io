Privacy Policy

How Dimensions Master handles your data and ensures your privacy.

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Overview

The Dimensions Master plugin for AutoCAD is developed by Ayman Amin. This privacy policy explains how user information is handled when using the application and complies with Autodesk’s Publisher requirements.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Dimensions Master does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user data, or drawing content. All calculations (such as measuring total lengths of lines, polylines, arcs, and splines) occur entirely within AutoCAD on the user's local machine.

The plugin does not use analytics, advertising networks, or any online tracking mechanisms. It performs operations only in memory and does not access, save, or share data externally.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Dimensions Master does not share any data with third parties, Autodesk, or affiliated entities. If the plugin ever integrates with a third party in the future, that party will be required to offer the same level of privacy protection as outlined in this policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because the plugin does not collect or store user data, no retention or deletion actions are required. All information processed by the plugin exists temporarily in memory during runtime and is cleared when AutoCAD is closed.

5. Revoking Consent and User Control

As the plugin does not collect or store data, users do not need to provide consent or manage personal information. If the plugin’s functionality changes in the future to include data handling, users will be notified and offered an opt-out mechanism.

6. Internet Access

Dimensions Master operates entirely offline. It does not connect to the Internet or transmit any information to external servers or services.

7. Policy Updates

If future versions of Dimensions Master introduce new features involving data usage or connectivity, this policy will be updated accordingly. Updates will be published at the official privacy policy URL.
